Detailed Description

T/F Invariant if FRIENDLY is closest to BALL.

T/F Invariant if size of TEAM FRIENDLY > TEAM ENEMY.

T/F Invariant if FRIENDLY has BALL.

T/F Invariant if ENEMY has BALL.

T/F Invariant if ENEMY does not have BALL.

T/F Invariant if the ball is on ENEMY side.

T/F Invariant if the ball is on FRIENDLY side.

T/F Invariant if a ROBOT has the BALL.

T/F Invariant if the ball is in our defense area and not moving.

Fuzzy Invariant how close the closest ENEMY is to the BALL Range [0->BALL_IS_CLOSE, BALL_IS_CLOSE*2]

Fuzzy Invariant how close the closest FRIENDLY is to the BALL Range [0->BALL_IS_CLOSE, BALL_IS_CLOSE*2]

Fuzzy Invariant based on the current speed of the ball Range [0->BALL_IS_MOVING_SLOW_LIMIT, BALL_GOT_SHOT_LIMIT]

Fuzzy Invariant how close the closest FRIENDLY is to the BALL Range [0, BALL_IS_CLOSE*4]

Fuzzy Invariant based on the average distance of the CLOSEST ENEMY to EACH FRIENDLY Range [0->DISTANCE_TO_ROBOT_CLOSE, DISTANCE_TO_ROBOT_FAR]

Fuzzy Invariant based on the openness of the ENEMY GOAL from BALL Range [0% , 100%]

Fuzzy Invariant based on the minimal openness of the GOAL from all FRIENDLY Range [0% , 100%]

Fuzzy Invariant based on the openness of the ENEMY GOAL from BALL Range [100% , 0%]
