A Grid is a 2D vector of Vector2 points. The points will be evenly spaced and centered in the given range.

Parameters
offSetXthe distance you want to horizontally shift the grid from 0 (where 0 is left edge)
offSetYthe distance you want to vertically shift the grid from 0 (where 0 is bottom edge)
regionWidththe length of the region you want the grid to encompass (x-direction)
regionHeightthe width of the region you want the grid to encompass (y-direction)
numStepsXnumber of segments to divide the grid into in x direction
numStepsYnumber of segments to divide the grid into in y direction
