Detailed Description

Singleton class that handles the communication between the Interface and the rest of the AI.

The communication is split into two parts: -> out: InterfacePublisher takes care of sending messages to the Interface. -> in: InterfaceSubscriber takes care of receiving messages from the Interface.

While both parts are relatively small and could be part of the InterfaceGateway, they are separated to make the data flow (hopefully) more clear.
